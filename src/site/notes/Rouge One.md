The first in my watch order for [[Star Wars\|Star Wars]]. This may seem like a weird place to start for [[Star Wars\|Star Wars]] fans but I think watching the original trilogy first is the best way to go and Rouge One leads in very nicely to [[A New Hope\|A New Hope]].

This is a very good film that has good stakes, introduces some key characters to the original trilogy very well and feels great to watch.

Unlike a lot of my other fan edit recommendations which I think are the best way to watch a lot of the films or series where I have recommended them I don't think this film needs the fan edit as much. However, there is a good fan edit called: [The Battle of Scarif](https://maple-films.com/rogue-one-fanedit) that really trims this down and just has the main battle at the end if you want a short mini film to watch before watching A New Hope.
