The best of the prequel trilogy films in my opinion. There are some great battle scenes in this one. Obi-Wan and Anakin are both at their best here. We also get a good tie up to lead into [[A New Hope\|A New Hope]]. All in all, a good film with some iconic moments. I still recommend the [Hal9000](https://originaltrilogy.com/topic/Some-info-help-for-the-Hal-9000-Edits-of-the-Prequel-Sequel-Trilogies-more/id/60855) edit called: [Labyrinth of Evil](https://originaltrilogy.com/topic/Star-Wars-Episode-III-Labyrinth-Of-Evil-Released/id/16454) for this one. The changes are reasonably light but work well, although do watch the alternate version if you want this to fit in with the Clone Wars series.
