Written: 02-10-2017

This is a great turn based strategy game with a compelling story as long as you don't mind reading. You are frequently unsure of your companions goals and have to consider every action carefully or risk losing supplies or characters. The fact that to promote characters they have to get the final hit on an enemy can be frustrating at times, but aside from this the game is very well polished and fun to play.
