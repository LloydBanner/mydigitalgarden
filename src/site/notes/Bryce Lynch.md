Bryce Lynch is a TTRPG Blogger who focuses on old school adventure reviews. He has a blog called [tenfootpole](https://tenfootpole.org/ironspike/) where he posts his reviews. He has some good principles for adventure design and his a great list of [adventure recommendations](https://tenfootpole.org/ironspike/?page_id=844).
