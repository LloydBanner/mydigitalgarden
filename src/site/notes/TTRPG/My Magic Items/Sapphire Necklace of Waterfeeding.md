Rarity: uncommon
Type: necklace
Sources: Lloyd's Brain

When worn the wearer has an uncontrollable urge to jump into bodies of water (save vs paralysis). The skin of the wearer absorbs water, filling them as if eating meal. Up to 10x the wearer's volume can be absorbed per day.
