### You each receive a letter that reads...
Dear {Character Name}

I am writing to you concerning your inheritance. A lady in the village of [Stagbrook](Stagbrook%20(tiny%20village).md) by the name of Lucinda Beestinger recently died. Unfortunately, all of her direct relatives died so you are one of twenty one who have a roughly equal claim on her rather meager estate. If you wish to claim your inheritance, please arrive in [Stagbrook](Stagbrook%20(tiny%20village).md) on the fifth day of the third month to claim it, if you do not arrive by then your share of the inheritance will be lost to those who do. When you arrive please find me, Barnaby Hope, I am the lawyer in charge of this case. 

DO NOT LOSE THIS LETTER! IT IS PROOF OF YOUR INHERTANCE!

Barnaby Hope
Lawyer, Writer, Entrepreneur
