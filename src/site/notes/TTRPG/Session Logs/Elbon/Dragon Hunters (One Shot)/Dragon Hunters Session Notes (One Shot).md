## Session Notes

**[[TTRPG/Elbon Setting/Calendar/Elbon Calendar\|Calendar]] Date:** 14-3-569

![Hazelmond.png](/img/user/z_assets/TTRPG/Place%20Pictures/Hazelmond/Hazelmond.png)
- The adventures arrived in Hazelmond after hearing of a reward for killing a nearby baby blue dragon 
- Grolf the [[TTRPG/My Monsters/Pigman\|Pigman]] village leader invited them into the town hall to eat and rest after the journey
- All the people in Hazelmond were [[TTRPG/My Monsters/Pigman\|Pigmen]] and the food looked and tasted awfully like pork.
- The party didn't eat much and Grolf was mildly annoyed by this
- They retired taking various huts around the village as shelter as many [[TTRPG/My Monsters/Pigman\|Pigmen]] had been taken so most were unoccupied. Grolf claims this was the dragon
- Dalgurin saw two [[TTRPG/My Monsters/Pigman\|Pigmen]] entering a hut through a window and woke Malvec. They went to investigate.
- Theysaw the two drag a [[TTRPG/My Monsters/Pigman\|Pigman]] body to the cellar of the town hall.
- The rest of the party were woken up
![Cellar of Grolf.png](/img/user/z_assets/TTRPG/Place%20Pictures/Hazelmond/Cellar%20of%20Grolf.png)
- They opened the cellar door and Malvec threw a torch down to see. It banged loudly and the two [[TTRPG/My Monsters/Pigman\|Pigmen]] attackers returned 
- The party tried to run but tumbled over each other on the stairs and the [[TTRPG/My Monsters/Pigman\|Pigmen]] got some attacks on them while they recovered
- Malvec ran up the stairs while everyone else fought 
- They made short work of the [[TTRPG/My Monsters/Pigman\|Pigmen]] with a sleep spell and tied them up, then put them in a cupboard for later
- In the next room were many bones piled up. Dalgurin poured water and found a pressure plate
- Nymeria used her wooden pole to press it and 3 skeletons formed from the bones and attacked
- The fight wasn't too difficult for the party
- They continued through the south door and found pig bodies hung on meat hooks
- Searching the room they found the belongings of the [[TTRPG/My Monsters/Pigman\|Pigmen]] who had been killed in a cupboard. There was treasure and an amulet that seems magical 
- Malvec put on the amulet and heared his mother's voice chiding him
- They continued, opening a door and finding Grolf chopping up a [[TTRPG/My Monsters/Pigman\|Pigman]]. Malvec tried to talk to him but he turned and said that he can't let him live now he knows what he is doing 
- The others left before Grolf saw them and use the other route to get into Grolf's room through a pantry of preserved meat
- Malvec closes the door behind him and held it while Grolf tried to break it down
- The others surprised Grolf opening the other door and attacking 
- When Grolf turned to fight them Malvec also enters attacking with his crowbar
- After the fight the party were injured but no one was dead.
- Dalgurin took Grolf's falchion which glows blue when it is raining
- They talked to the captured [[TTRPG/My Monsters/Pigman\|Pigmen]], killing one to make the other talk. They found out about Grolf's obsession with [[TTRPG/My Monsters/Pigman\|Pigman]] meat and that most of the villagers don't know. 
- After a rest, the party took the captured [[TTRPG/My Monsters/Pigman\|Pigman]] and made him tell the villagers what happened. They gave the party the 1500gp reward that was for the dragon for stopping Grolf
- They then went to investigate the Dragon anyway. A girl in town had seen a cave where she thought the dragon was so she and her father guided them
- After a short journey they found the cave. Inside was a sleeping baby blue dragon called Scaelith
- Malvec woke it at spoke Draconic to it. It was friendly and spoke common too
- They explained that they had stopped Grolf who wanted Scaelith dead
- Scaelith was thankful and gave them some of her gold for preventing her trouble in the future 
- They returned to Hazelmond
