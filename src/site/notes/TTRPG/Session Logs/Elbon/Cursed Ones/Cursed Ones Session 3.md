## Session Notes

![illusionistTower.webp](/img/user/z_assets/TTRPG/Place%20Pictures/Whiterock/illusionistTower.webp)

- Some of the party awoke in the Gawping Cod tavern as they others returned from the sewers
- Not everyone fancied going to the council meeting, but Elfis, Robin, Danny and Ferdinand did
- Before going they stopped by the shops and bought a few items
- When they reached the tower where the illusionists commanded Whiterock from they were escorted to the council room by a guard
- Robin Houses managed to slip away to go and investigate the tower as Danny made a comment on one of the statues
- Robin quickly made his way up to the second floor while the others were interrogated about their intentions in Whiterock
- Robin was in a library but couldn't read. He then tried to go further up the stairs to find the orb of power but found himself in a store cupboard due to some illusionist trickery
- Hamish the illusionist mostly interrogate the others. Each said they were just passing through, or were a humble merchant or something similar. The illusionists couldn't argue with this but were not happy. They gave the party a long list of things to collect as a job to keep them busy
- One of the illusionists also noticed the bark growing on the arms of each of the party and recognised it as the Curse of Barkskin Binding. They said for fulfilling the task they could cure them
- Around this time Robin was spotted leaving the store cupboard by a guard and escorted to the council chamber
- He was ask the same questions as the other and gave equally vague responses
- They were also asked about their travelling companions who were still at the Gawping Cod
- After they had discussed this they asked for a tour of the tower to see if there was any "information" that they could gain about the items they needed to collect
- Vastrasa another illusionist from the council offered to help with this and escorted them up the tower
