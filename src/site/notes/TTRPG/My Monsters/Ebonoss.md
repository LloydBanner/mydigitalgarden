# Ebonoss

An Ebonoss is a dark shadow of a creature with no true form. It forms around the bones of dead creatures turning them black and bringing them to life. It is made of a constantly moving black oil like substance.

- **Bone Possession:** Can't deal damage when not possessing any bones. Possesses Bones by enveloping them.
- **Shatter:** Can shatter a limb by slamming it into the ground. 1d8 damage + 1 damage to all other characters in 10ft.
- **Immunities:** Immune to sleep spells and non magical attacks.

---
## Tactics
- Will try to possess bones and surprise PCs on first attack
- Will seep out of bones when all limbs are shattered or can no longer move and try to get away or posses other bones
- Always tries to position itself where flailing it's limbs will hit multiple targets, if it can't it will do a shatter attack on one enemy as long as this doesn't incapacitate it or there are other bones to possess.
- If retreating will do a shatter attack before leaving the host
