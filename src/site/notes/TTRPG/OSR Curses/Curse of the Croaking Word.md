**Effect:** Each day, the cursed PC must choose one word they’re not allowed to say (DM can pick randomly too). If they say it, they croak like a frog and are silenced for 1d4 rounds.

**Escalation:** The taboo words get more common or hard to avoid (like “the,” “and,” or a party member’s name).

**Why it's good:** Makes players think before speaking, adds lighthearted danger to dialogue. Great for bard-types or talkative characters.
