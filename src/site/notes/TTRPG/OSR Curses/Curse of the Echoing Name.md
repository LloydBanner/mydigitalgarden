**Effect:** Every time the cursed character says their own name, it echoes unnaturally loud, so loud it risks alerting enemies nearby (1-in-6 chance per instance if hiding).

**Escalation:** After a few days, this includes any _mention_ of their name by others, and eventually writing it down.

**Why it's good:** It’s disruptive in just the right way, forces clever workaround or motivates them to find a cure to avoid screwing up stealth or diplomacy.
