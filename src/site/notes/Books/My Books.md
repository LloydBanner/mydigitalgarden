## A Curse Upon Mistrun
Released: August 2021

This one is medieval fantasy. The unique element is that it is set on a land which is on a giant rock that is floating through a void filled with dense mist. This land moves following a repeating cycle and occasionally meets other lands. The different houses are all on edge after a recent civil war and mythical monsters start to return causing dismay wherever they go. ## Crumbling World
Released: September 2018

The first story that I published. It is about a man traveling to a distant planet to make first contact with an alien species. When he arrives the world he is visiting has been practically destroyed and he has to survive alone. I wrote this one over the summer between my first and second year of university and I think a lot of this comes from my experiences living away from home for the first time.
