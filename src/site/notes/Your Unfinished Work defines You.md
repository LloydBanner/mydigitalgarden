[Crystal](https://www.youtube.com/@888_crystal) does an excellent job of explaining why you shouldn't always finish your creative work. I really like this principle and think the idea of leaving work unfinished is good. It allows you to move to what inspires you and sometimes you just realise that an idea just isn't that good when you start it. This inspired me to make my [[Creative Graveyard\|Creative Graveyard]] to showcase some of my unfinished work.
