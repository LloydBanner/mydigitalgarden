Written 25-06-2024

This DLC has a massive open world bigger than most games. Yes the bosses are kind of hard, but I think I was a bit rusty after not playing the game for 2 years. Once you get back into it, the difficult is close to the later sections of the base game I would say. Take your time, find the new items that increase your stats relative to the DLC enemies and you will quickly be having a good time. Great, huge DLC that is well worth the money.
