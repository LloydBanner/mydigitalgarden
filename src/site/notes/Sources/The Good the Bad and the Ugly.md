I have been making my way through the Clint Eastwood western trilogy over the last year or so. They hold up particularly well, and are always fun to watch. I watched this on the flight to Türkiye when I was going on holiday. Definitely a good way to pass a plane journey. I feel like I had seen the end before though so I must have caught that on TV at some point. It's well shot, stylish, fun and knows what it wants to be. For a film that is over half a century old it has aged incredibly well. This [[Sources/A Fistfull of Dollars\|A Fistfull of Dollars]] and [[Sources/For a Few Dollars More\|For a Few Dollars More]] are all great westerns. The only others that I have enjoyed as much are [[Sources/Rango\|Rango]] and [[Sources/Django Unchained\|Django Unchained]].
