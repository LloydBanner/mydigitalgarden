Before reading this book I had already started to believe that living a more minimalist life was worthwhile. Spark joy made me realise that I didn't need to hold onto things that weren't making me happy. After reading this book and putting some ideas into practice, I now have a less cluttered and more focused life, if not entirely minimalist.
