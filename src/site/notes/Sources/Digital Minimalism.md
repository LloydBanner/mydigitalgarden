I first read this at the end of my second year of university. Afterwards I deleted a lot of my social media and felt much better for it. I now try to have intentionally with my digital and non-digital life. This and [[Sources/Exurb1a\|Exurb1a's]] videos had a big influence on this for me. These concepts are always on my mind and it is probably about time I gave this book another read.

The idea of intentionally with technology has had a big impact on me and was one of the core ideas when I was writing [[Main Notes/Life is Better this Way\|Life is Better this Way]], Digital Minimalism likely had an impact on this.
