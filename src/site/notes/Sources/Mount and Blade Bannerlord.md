Bannerlord is good, it expands on most elements from [[Sources/Mount and Blade\|Mount and Blade]] and the other [[Sources/Mount and Blade\|Mount and Blade]] games while adding new features such as siege equipment. This game also looks amazing considering the sheer number of characters on screen. I last played this in early access when it was a little rough but will certainly return in the future.
