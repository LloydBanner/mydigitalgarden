Delicious in Dungeon is an anime series which is heavily inspired by D&D and other fantasy [[Main Notes/TTRPG\|TTRPGs]]. The main concept is that the adventurers have to eat the monsters in the dungeon to survive long enough to save their friend because they don't have enough food. This concept is executed really well and leads to some great moments. Absolutely worth a watch.
