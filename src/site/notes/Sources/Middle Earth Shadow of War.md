The sequel to [[Sources/Middle Earth Shadow of Mordor\|Middle Earth Shadow of Mordor]] that greatly expands the nemesis system from the first game. When it came out, the game was badly affected by microtransactions but these have now been removed so I can happily recommend the game. I also recommend playing on the brutal difficulty where both you and orcs have lower health because this creates more nemesis system interactions. A fun game, but I still feel like there is much more potential with the nemesis system.
