When I first watched this I thought it was overrated. That the stories didn't fit together and come to a good conclusion and that although it was well made it had missed something.

Upon rewatching I thought this film was amazing. It has gone from the middle of my list of my favourite [[Sources/Quentin Tarantino\|Quentin Tarantino]] films to the top. It is well shot, funny, has great dialogue and the stories intertwine incredibly well. I don't know what 20 year old me was thinking. Great film.
