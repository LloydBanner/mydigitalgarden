I have always loved monster manuals for RPGs. This one is packed with great ideas, unique creatures and fun drawings. You can get the [PDF](https://www.drivethrurpg.com/en/product/147588/cc1-creature-compendium) for free and the paperback is also pretty cheap. It is also compatible with pretty much all old school fantasy RPGs.
