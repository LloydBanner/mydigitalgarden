Warband still has great gameplay. The main downside from the later entry [[Sources/Mount and Blade Bannerlord\|Mount and Blade Bannerlord]] is the now more dated graphics. On the other hand, due to this being a much older game and no longer getting updates from the developers, the game has many many mods. These range from total conversions such as [Last Days of the Third Age](https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=299974223) a [[Sources/The Lord of the Rings\|Lord of the Rings]] total conversion mod to small tweaks and fixes. These mods are certainly worth looking into if you like the game.
