The first game in the criminally underrated [[Sources/Banner Saga Trilogy\|Banner Saga Trilogy]]. These games are works of art. The sprites and scenes are all beautifully made. The story is incredible and the strategy aspects feel like you are fighting for every inch which is tonally consistent with the story. An all around great game that deserves to be played if you like turn based games and Norse inspired settings. Possibly slightly weaker than the first game.
