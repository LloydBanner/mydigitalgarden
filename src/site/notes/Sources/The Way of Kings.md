An excellent fantasy epic. I think Kaladin's struggle throughout this book, from becoming a slave to learning and mastering his abilities, is [[Sources/Brandon Sanderson\|Brandon Sanderson]] best story telling and some of the best I have seen in general. The incredible world building on top of that creates a truly great story and book series.
