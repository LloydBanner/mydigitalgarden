A great game and follow up to [[Sources/Ico\|Ico]]. It has a lot of the atmosphere of [[Sources/Ico\|Ico]], but instead you of being puzzle focused it has you fight a series of bosses. You have to climb along these giants to find their weak spots. This creates some great moments.
