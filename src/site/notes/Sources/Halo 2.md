Halo 2 expanded on a lot of mechanics in [[Sources/Halo Combat Evolved\|Halo Combat Evolved]] and refined the formula. The mechanics from this game stuck around mostly for [[Sources/Halo 3\|Halo 3]] and [[Sources/Halo ODST\|Halo ODST]] with a few more changes in [[Sources/Halo Reach\|Halo Reach]]. This one suffers a little from long repeating sections and sometimes unfair difficulty on hard settings. I would recommend playing this one on normal difficulty with some skulls or on heroic. This one is very much not fun on legendary.

[[Sources/The Master Chief Collection\|The Master Chief Collection]] is the best way to play this now.
