[Raiding the Obsidian Keep](https://www.themerrymushmen.com/product/raiding-the-obsidian-keep/) is an adventure for [[Main Notes/OSE\|OSE]]. It is designed for players level 2-4 and was published by [The Merry Mushmen](https://www.themerrymushmen.com/).

I enjoyed playing this. It has nice concise descriptions with lots of interesting items, rooms and places to work with. My main issue was the large number of NPCs which all of my players want to interrogate thoroughly about what was going on with the keep. Other than this I had no problems. It is easy to pick up and run, doesn't require too much prep, has a fun story and fit nicely into my ongoing campaign. I'd highly recommend it.
