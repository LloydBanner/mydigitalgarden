[[Sources/Skerples\|Skerples]] has written an incredible book here that really digs into making monsters in you roleplaying games interesting and unique. It has a bunch of random tables to generate unique encounters with all of your favourite fantasy creatures and is compatible with most old school [[Main Notes/TTRPG\|TTRPG]] systems.
