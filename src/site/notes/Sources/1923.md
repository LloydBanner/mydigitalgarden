A great TV shows and western. I enjoy this much more than [[Sources/Yellowstone\|Yellowstone]] and [[Sources/1883\|1883]] which were also good. [[Sources/1883\|1883]] felt like it could have more depth while [[Sources/Yellowstone\|Yellowstone]] felt like the ending was a little rough. 1923 tells a great self contained story and is enjoyable throughout. 

I did think a lot about the death at the end and what the meaning of this was, and I think it is trying to say that nature always wins. That humans do not have as much control as they think and that we should be careful in this world. I'm going to take it that way anyway.
