This game has the best sword combat I have seen in a VR game. It is very fluid and feels amazing, but may worry you a little if you enjoy it too much. There is a campaign mode that has recently been added but you can also just fight in an arena against waves of enemies. Definitely try it if you have a VR headset.

## Mods
I love the base game of Blade and Sorcery but I love [The Outer Rim](https://www.nexusmods.com/bladeandsorcery/mods/528) mod more. It adds practically every [[Star Wars\|Star Wars]] weapon you can imagine to the game and waves of enemies with lightsabers. It even gives you the ability to use the force. As far as I am concerned this is more fun than any Star Wars VR game although there are a few good ones. Add this and a few mods that add different Star Wars enemy waves and you will have a blast.
