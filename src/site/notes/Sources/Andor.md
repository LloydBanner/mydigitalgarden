The first in my watch order for [[Sources/Star Wars\|Star Wars]]. This may seem like a weird place to start for [[Sources/Star Wars\|Star Wars]] fans but I think watching the original trilogy first is the best way to go and Andor and [[Rouge One \|Rouge One ]]lead in very nicely to [[Sources/A New Hope\|A New Hope]].

Andor is a great [[Sources/Star Wars\|Star Wars]] series that ties into [[Sources/Rouge One\|Rouge One]] incredibly well. Throughout Andor you get a slow build up in tension until it feels like it is about to burst and then in [[Sources/Rouge One\|Rouge One]] it does. This feeling that flows through these pieces of media is expertly crafted and create a great prequel to [[Sources/A New Hope\|A New Hope]]. The series makes minor details from [[Sources/Rouge One\|Rouge One]] and the original [[Sources/Star Wars\|Star Wars]] trilogy feel far more significant and does politics very well. I had a few issues with things not tying up perfectly if you focus too much on the details and some of the tech feeling a little less '[[Sources/Star Wars\|Star Wars]]' than I would like but this is still absolutely worth a watch.
