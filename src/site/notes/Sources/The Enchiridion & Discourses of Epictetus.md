I found the discourses to be very varied, some resonated while others felt silly or like they contradicted others. I like the idea of accepting that you shouldn't worry about things not in your power, but this is taken to the extreme almost removing emotion from life. The enchiridion was more consistent and for the most part had a better way of presenting the ideas. I may come back to the enchiridion but not the whole book.
