The author was born in 1916 and educated at the University of California, first as a mining engineer, then majoring in physics and finally in journalism. During the 1940s and 1950s, he contributed widely to science fiction and fantasy magazines. His first novel, [[Sources/The Dying Earth\|The Dying Earth]] , was published in 1950 to great acclaim.
