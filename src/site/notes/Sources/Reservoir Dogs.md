One of my favourite [[Sources/Quentin Tarantino\|Quentin Tarantino]] films.

I first watched this quite some time ago. Rewatching it now it was still great and gripping. This is impressive since there is actually very little action in the film and most of this tension is created through dialogue between the characters. I also like the way it has a nonlinear narrative that all comes together well in the end.

I have been planning to write some fantasy short stories but want to interconnect them to feed towards a greater narrative. I had remembered this film doing that more but it didn't do as much of this as I had remembered. It still does it and has given me some ideas but I think I am still missing a piece of the puzzle.
