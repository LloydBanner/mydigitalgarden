Niccolò di Bernardo dei Machiavelli was an Italian political philosopher, musician, poet, and playwright. He is a figure of the Italian Renaissance and a central figure of its political component, most widely known for his treatises on realist political theory ([[Sources/The Prince\|The Prince]]) on the one hand and republicanism (_Discourses on Livy_) on the other.
