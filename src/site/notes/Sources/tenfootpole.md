Tenfootpole is a [[Main Notes/TTRPG\|TTRPG]] blogger who reviews and plays old school [[Main Notes/TTRPG\|TTRPG]] adventures. He is generally well regarded and his: [My Favourite of the New Old School Adventures](https://tenfootpole.org/ironspike/?page_id=844) page is a great place to start if you want to find some good adventures. [His Blog](https://tenfootpole.org/ironspike/) generally is updated regularly and it is a good source for any OSR DM.
