This is the most recent Hitman trilogy compiled into one game with a much smaller file size. I think this is the best way to play Hitman and this plays better than almost all the old games.

Each level is a semi open world sandbox where you can equip disguises, use gadgets (both goofy and serious), and infiltrate locations to hunt down your targets in anyway you see fit. You can play and try to never been seen, or be a little less careful and both ways are great fun. There are also a few scripted assassination for each of your targets which are fun to preform.

With all the maps there is tonnes of equipment to unlock, and approaches to explore. Even if you just play through the main missions there is plenty to do, but there are also escalations which are progressively hard challenges, small challenges in each level for unlocks and the freelancer mode which is a sort of roguelike mode.
