I liked the idea and story of this game but while playing I always felt the combat was much less fluid that the Jedi Knight games. When [[Sources/Jedi Survivor\|Jedi Survivor]] the sequel to this came out I realised that this may have been intentional. Kal is meant to be weak and slowly become a Jedi by the end of the game and this is presented through gameplay as well as story.

I imagine Playing this and then Survivor afterwards flows very well.
