A tower defence game where you use plants to defend yourself from Zombies.

A game that has aged perfectly. It is very well balanced, is easy to learn, has various fun modes and challenges. It does just about everything you can do with the concept of a tower defence game where you use plants to stop zombies from trying to invade your house. It is great, everyone should try it. It is also quite fun to try and get all the achievements. I highly recommend it.
