Unlike a lot of my other fan edit recommendations, which I think are the best way to watch a lot of the films or series where I have recommended them, I don't think this film needs the fan edit as much. However [The Battle of Scarif](https://maple-films.com/rogue-one-fanedit) really trims [[Sources/Rouge One\|Rouge One]] down and just has the main battle at the end if you want a short mini film to watch before watching [[Sources/A New Hope\|A New Hope]].
