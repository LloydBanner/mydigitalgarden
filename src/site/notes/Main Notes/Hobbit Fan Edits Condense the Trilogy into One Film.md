I found the Hobbit trilogy lacking when I watched it. It is stretched a little thin and there is too much filler for my liking. I highly recommend fan edits that edit them down into one film such as the [Ed Edit](https://youtu.be/QFnvzHgF5aM?si=NCiFwh54YLiYbc9G) or the [Maple Films Tolkien Edit](http://www.maple-films.com/downloads.html). These feel much better to watch and still have all the best bits from the films.
