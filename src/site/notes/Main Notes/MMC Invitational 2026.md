On the 14th of March 2026 I will be running a gaming tournament for my birthday. If I have sent you a link to this page you are invited. Below are the games we will be playing with the conditions we will be playing them in if you want to practice (you don't have to this is for fun). We can order some food somewhere along the way too.
# Games:
### Halo 3
**We will discover our FPS champions with Halo 3. The ultimate MLG game of the 2000s. Make a pair and see if you can reach the final. No Mountain Dew sponsorship unfortunately.**

- **Rules:** Slayer 2v2, Assault Rifle, Magnum, 2 x frags to start. 10 minute games, first to 15 kills.
- **Allowed Maps:** MLG Construct 8, Epitaph, MLG Guardian 8, MLG Narrows 8, Snowbound, MLG Pit 8, Foundry, MLG Amplified 8, MLG Onslaught 8, Blackout, Could Storage, Assembly, Citadel, MLG Heretic 8, Icebox (Trying to stick to smaller maps)
- **Tournament Conditions:** Pair up with a partner, Best of 3 against another team, alternate which team picks maps. Winning team goes through to the next round. 

### Super Smash Bros. Ultimate
**Who is the Ultimate Smash Bro? Let's find out with a tournament. Make sure you have some practice with your main, or don't.**

- **Rules:** 1v1 3 stock, 10 minute games, no Items, no final smash
- **Allowed Stages:** Any
- **Tournament Conditions:** Best of 3 against another player, alternate which player picks maps. Winning player goes through to the next round. 

### Ultimate Chicken Horse
**Finally we have our wildcard game. Ultimate Chicken Horse. Build a platformer level, together, which hopefully you can beat and no one else can.**

- **Rules:** Up to 4 player free for all, Maximum time of 15 minutes, 30 seconds to place items, 90 seconds to complete round
- **Allowed Stages:** Any
- **Tournament Conditions:** Players vote on the stage they want, play whichever map the game picks. Winning player goes through to the next round.
