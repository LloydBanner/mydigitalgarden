Released: September 2018

The first story that I published. It is about a man traveling to a distant planet to make first contact with an alien species. When he arrives the world he is visiting has been practically destroyed and he has to survive alone. I wrote this one over the summer between my first and second year of university and I think a lot of this comes from my experiences living away from home for the first time.
