I think one line can sometimes conjure up many possibilities in your mind. so here is a
list of quotes to get your imagination going.

1. "You've never seen a greater hive of scum and villainy." - [[Sources/A New Hope\|A New Hope]]
2. "You are a sad, strange, little man, and you have my pity. Farewell." - [[Toy Story\|Toy Story]]
3. "My name is xxxx. You killed my father. Prepare to die." - The Princess Bride
4. "It's just a flesh wound." - Monty Python and the Holy Grail
5. "I’m going to make him an offer he can’t refuse." - The Godfather
6. "I do wish we could chat longer, but...I'm having an old friend for dinner. Bye." - The Silence of the Lambs
7. “So this is how liberty dies...with thunderous applause.” [[Sources/Revenge of the Sith\|Revenge of the Sith]]
8. "I see dead people." - The Sixth Sense
9. "It's only after we've lost everything that we're free to do anything" - [[Sources/Fight Club (film)\|Fight Club]]
