I went back to Constellation. They seemed somewhat surprised but were happy to have me back. I went out into the galaxy looking for the artifacts which even after seeing their reactions with each other I thought were useless. We have six in total now and when another was added they floated together spinning to create a globe like shape. I thought it was just a useless affect of some old metals but now I know it's something more.

Through working with Constellation, I have gotten to know a lot of their members. I have become particularly fond of Andreja, and we spend a lot of time working together. She also has a more troubled past and is trying to work things through. We make quite the pair. Recently, we were exploring a planet with a stronger reading that had previously been overlooked by constellation. On the planet, we found a huge temple and much larger versions of the artefacts floating on their own.

Inside the structure, there was no gravity and a completed artefact structure. Distortion of light appeared around the globe of artefacts, and as I used my spacesuit to jet to them, the rings in the centre began to spin faster and faster. I continued to move through each of the lights until the artefacts lined up to create a huge portal-like ring. I floated into the centre, and there was a sudden flash of colour. When I could see again, I was outside the structure.

That wasn't all, though. I felt different. Acting upon the feeling, I cast out my hand and the snow at the place where I pointed began to float. It had lost gravity. Shortly after, it floated back to the ground. Andreja was surprised but also overjoyed that we had finally found something different. We made our way back to the Lodge, the constellation headquarters on New Atlantis. There, I showed the others my abilities, and they all had renewed faith in their quest.

Walter, the Staud-Eklund CEO, finally seemed to have some faith in me and asked me and Andreja to come on a mission woth him. We made our way to Neon where we were to make a trade for an artifact. It turned out the artifact had been stolen from one of Walter's competitors. He impounded the Hyena and set his soldiers on us. To avoid the problem we snook into his tower and negotiated a deal with him.

Thinking we were free, we took off into orbit. However, that wasn't the end of our troubles. In orbit, we were hailed by someone who claimed to be "starborn" and said we should stop hunting the artefacts. The ship they were in did not look human, but also didn't have a significantly different structure on scanners. Either way, it looked like it would destroy the Hyena in a fight. I hit the grav drive and we jumped to New Atlantis moon just before they opened fire. Luckily, the speedy input had only taken us one celestial body away from our destination.

Back in the lodge we discussed the starborn. When they hailed they knew about constellation and wanted us to hand over all our atifacts but would not say why. We were all conflicted on who they were, if they were human or not and what to do about them. One thing we did know though was that we would need to be more careful. These starborn were dangerous. With them and the bounty hunters still on my tail it seems I will never catch a break.
