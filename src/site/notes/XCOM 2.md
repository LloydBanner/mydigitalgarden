XCOM 2 expands on [[XCOM\|XCOM]], but continues as if you had lost in the first game which very few games do. The aliens have taken over Earth and you play a guerrilla rebel force trying to strike back at these alien overlords. Another great game in the series.

## Mods
There is also a great mod for this one that replaces the aliens with the Empire from [[Star Wars\|Star Wars]] if you like that sort of thing called: [Advent to Empire](https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1395522281).
